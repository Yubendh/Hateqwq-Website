About Me

Welcome to my corner of the web! I'm Days, also known as HateQWQ. I've been passionate about programming and technology for as long as I can remember.

As a peripheral enthusiast, I spend my time exploring the latest in mice, keyboards, mousepads, and audio. I enjoy testing different setups, finding new and interesting peripherals from different parts of the world to share with everyone.

As a future software developer, currently studying Software Development in University, I aim to innovate as well as incorporate my love for tech in future projects.

When I'm not coding or testing new peripherals, you might find me gaming, aim training, as well as consistently participating in discussions about peripherals on multiple platforms!

Feel free to explore my website to learn more about my projects, reviews, and thoughts on peripherals. If you have any questions or would like to collaborate, don't hesitate to reach out!
