Hello! I am Days.

Based in Phnom Penh, Cambodia, I'm currently a Software Development Major at the American University of Phnom Penh.

My passion lies in collecting computer peripherals like mice, keyboards, mousepads, and audio.

I also love yapping on Twitter about peripherals, you can also find me on Youtube posting keyboard sound tests and various peripheral reviews.

Outside of that, I love tea, coding, listening to various music genres, as well as sleeping, ALOT OF SLEEPING.

What I Do

I review peripherals such as mice, mousepads, and keyboards, putting them through rigorous testing. I also like discussing peripherals with everyone on different platforms.

You can often find me on Discord or Twitter discussing & posting about peripherals and new releases. On top of that I can also help you pick out peripherals according to your needs.

My Vision

I aim to not only review & talk about products that are avaliable globally, but also introduce everyone to sides of the peripheral community that most have never explored before.

Promoting products from Taobao & individual sellers and enthusiasts from Xianyu which of whom sell interesting products that I feel everyone should know about.

On top of that, I want my reviews to be informative and help guide you during your next purchase of a certain peripheral.
